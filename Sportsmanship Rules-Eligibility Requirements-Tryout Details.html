GSE Sportsmanship Rules Full Rules Can Be Found Here All participants are expected to follow the GLHF pledge while participating in all GSE events: As part of the GLHF Pledge, we promise to: Be a good sport whether I win or lose. Know that people online are real people and my words have real impact. Set a positive example with my behavior. Speak up against discrimination, hate speech, harassment, and abuse. Show integrity by honoring the rules, my opponents, and my teammates. Stop, listen, and reassess if Im told that my words or actions are harmful. Respect others, even if their sincere opinions are different from my own. This pledge is not just to be followed by players, but club advisors, casters, and all members of your esports program and community.

GSE Eligibility Requirements A school or Community Organization may enter one (1) team in a Championship Season, though there are special circumstances when schools may enter more than one team. These circumstances will be announced via email or Discord when they arise. A school or Community Organization may enter as many teams into Contenders League Season that they can support playing simultaneously. A Club Advisor must be present at all Championship Season matches and will be the point of contact for opposing Club Advisors and Garden State Esports. Club Advisors are the responsible adult in charge of a team and ensuring compliance to these and all other rules.

Tryout Details Tryouts will be individual all applicants must understand that the coaches will select what team will compete the best.   If you already know that you cannot dedicate yourself to Thursdays in the Winter due to other commitments please do not try out.
